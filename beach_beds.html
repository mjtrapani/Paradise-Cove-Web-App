Terms of Enjoyment

Only Beer & Wine are allowed on the Beach! No kegs or Drinking Games,
Open Fires or any type of BBQs are Forbidden!!!
Smoking of Anything is not allowed here or any Beach in Malibu.

Please be a good neighbor No Loud Music.
We are a family friendly beach so please act accordingly.
We try to recycle everything so please help us keep Paradise Beautiful.
